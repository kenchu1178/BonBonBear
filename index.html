About us

BonBonBear Information Co., Ltd. was established in 2015 and we are very familiar with hardware, software and networking. Due to high quality requirements and good communication skills. Many business users can confidently hand over the project to us for development.

The company's latest product is an APP that can help people practice pronunciation in different languages. Learning a new language has always been very difficult, and this product can make learning easier, more fun, more perseverance.

The companies we work with are Showtime Cinemas, Capital Securities Corporation, CTBC Bank, BNP Paribas, Wistron ITS, Unicharm Corporation, Flytech Technology, Linksys Inc, SuperTron, Sapcious, AiBaDi Entertainment and so on.

The solutions we used are dotnet, Java, Unity, react native, cordova, xamarin, sql, mongoDB, heroku. For the requirements in IOT device. We also help to develop firmware and device driver, and deploy the server in Amazon or Azure.
